About Kallath's Foods

Our Story

Kallath's Food, based in Pilathara, Kannur, brings you the finest, authentic spices without preservatives. We take pride in offering high-quality products that enhance the flavors of your favorite dishes.

Our special items include traditional sambar and rasam powders, chutney powders, and a variety of other spices that will make your meals unforgettable.
